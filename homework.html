Level 4: Dialogue Comprehension

Task 4: Read the dialogue and answer the questions

Read the dialogue carefully and answer the questions below.

Wenjing: Hello, I'm Wenjing. I'm Chinese. I'm a teacher.

Answer the questions:

1. Where is Raj from?

2. What does Wenjing do?
